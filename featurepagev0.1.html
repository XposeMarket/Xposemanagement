Messages

Mobile first ▾

Message customers directly from the app with a mobile-first experience that looks and feels like a native SMS app. Threads sync across devices and with your shop's Twilio number so conversations stay in one place. Perfect for confirmations, reminders, and quick customer chats, all searchable and logged on the customer profile.

Works great on phones with full-screen chat, quick replies, and tappable phone numbers.

Appointments and Scheduling

Flow ready ▾

Book appointments easily, view your calendar at a glance, and keep track of every job. Clear scheduling visibility helps your team stay organized and prepared for every day.

Invoices and Payments

Clean totals ▾

Create professional invoices, track payments, and manage your shop’s revenue with ease. Everything is organized and accessible from one dashboard.

Jobs Management

Organized ▾

Track every job from start to finish, update statuses, assign staff, and keep your workflow organized. The jobs page makes it easy to manage work orders and ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

Calendar Day Colors

At a glance ▾

Quickly see your shop's status at a glance. Color-coded calendar days show busy periods, appointments, and open slots so you can plan ahead.

Day Details

Instant view ▾

Click any day to instantly view all jobs, appointments, and notes for that date. Stay organized and know exactly what's happening in your shop today.

Customer Profiles

Context ▾

Keep customer contact details, vehicle history, and previous appointments/invoices in one place to provide faster, more personalized service.

Inventory Management

Parts ready ▾

Track items your shop keep in stock. The Inventory page makes it easy to update any items/parts you have across your shop. Low-stock alerts and folder organization make reordering simple.

Revenue and Reporting

Profit aware ▾

Track revenue with built-in profit awareness. Compare gross revenue against staff pay and parts cost to understand true margins and make smarter pricing and staffing decisions.

Quick Appointment Entry

Fast input ▾

Create appointments quickly using our streamlined modal. Capture customer details, select services, and confirm availability in seconds.
