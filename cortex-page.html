Cortex is the Decision Engine
Behind Smarter Auto Shops

Cortex helps shops think through repairs the way experienced techs do. It connects diagnostics, symptoms, labor logic, and service context into clear workflows that remove guesswork without slowing anyone down.

Why Cortex Exists

Most shops are forced to piece together diagnostics from out-dated & disconnected sources. Generic code descriptions, static charts, forum guesses, and tribal knowledge.

Costing them time and money that they dont even realize - just because they're used to it. Cortex replaces that chaos with structure.

Instead of asking technicians to interpret raw information, Cortex organizes it into logical steps, probable causes, and confirmable outcomes. The result is faster decisions, fewer mistakes, and more confident estimates.

Cortex Analysis

Right off the bat - when you search & open a DTC code, symptom or any sort of workload, Cortex fires up. Cortex scans through its database of sources to give the users the most common cause for the selected vehicle - along with more likely causes + additional diagnosis steps, what the results of the diagnosis mean along with recommended services.

Cortex Labor Service Flow

When looking for services, Cortex references OEM and OEM+ procedures alongside trusted technician sources to surface the most accurate service operations. It accounts for required tools, service steps, and real-world labor context so advisors can build estimates that are clear, defensible, and easy for customers to understand.

Cortex Smart Diagnosis

Smart Diagnosis focuses on elimination and confirmation. Cortex helps identify whether an issue is mechanical, electrical, software-related, or service-based before unnecessary work is performed.

Why Cortex Feels Different

Built for consistency

Cortex ensures the same vehicle concern produces the same diagnostic logic, regardless of who is working the job.

Designed for real workflows

Outputs are structured for repair orders, not textbooks. Every step supports the way shops actually operate.

Confidence over guesswork

Cortex prioritizes confirmable steps and verification, reducing comebacks and improving trust with customers.

Built Into Xpose Management

Cortex is not an add-on or a separate system. It is fully embedded inside Xpose Management, enhancing every job, estimate, and decision without extra complexity.
