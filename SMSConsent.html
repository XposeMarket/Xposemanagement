Important Information

Who sends the messages?

Messages are sent by Xpose Management, including messages sent on behalf of participating businesses using the Xpose platform.

What messages will I receive?

Messages may include appointment reminders, confirmations, service updates, invoice notifications, and two-way customer support communications.

Is consent required?

Yes. Consent is required to receive SMS messages. Consent is not a condition of purchase and can be withdrawn at any time.

How do I stop messages?

Reply STOP at any time. You may receive a final confirmation message acknowledging your opt-out.

Do message rates apply?

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

What if I need help?

Reply HELP or contact support through the Xpose Management website.

Is my information stored?

Contact information and messaging preferences may be stored to manage communication preferences and provide service-related messaging.

Delivery disclaimer

Message delivery is subject to carrier availability and is not guaranteed.
